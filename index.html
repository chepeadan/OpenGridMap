About

We are an open community that crowdsources realistic power grid data to be used for research purposes. Here you will find the tools for crowdsourcing power grid data. The goal is to create an open platform for inferring realistic power grids based on actual data. Our vision is to provide a tool to researchers and practitioners that is able to produce realistic input data for simulation studies. OpenGridMap will support the entire process from data collection to formatting grid data for various purposes. We explore innovative ways to capture data and produce power grid approximations, e.g., using smartphone apps, drones, expert classification, existing map APIs, and graph inference algorithms. We are still in the early developing phase and we could really use your help. Join us and lets map the world's power grid!!! Check out: facebook page and the OpenGridMap App

Grid models

We have inferred models of the high voltage power grid for several countries for Matla/Simulink. Feel free to contact us with questions and request. Try them out and help us improve them: models.

How to help

  • Interested: Testing our App and giving feedback, Crowdsourcing data using our Android app (OpenGridMap App).

  • Researcher: Inference algorithms, contribute to development and become part of our organization (@opengridmap).

  • Utility: Distribution grid data sets for comparison with our inference, rules and criteria used to design, distribution grids.
